ABOUT

When Ivotron began, I was a kid in college trying to get better at photography, and socialize. I accomplished one of those things.
I attended conventions with the notion of establishing a brand, and to be honest, it was working.
The cosplay community is wonderful. It's a supportive community that made it easy to approach someone and ask if you could photograph them.
Despite that, I couldn't do it anymore, not because I didn't want to but because attending various cons just wasn't viable. I had no money.
That being said, I would still love to shoot with anyone who'd like to. You just let me know, and we can work something out.

Recently, I decided to make Ivotron into an alternate persona where I can
share thoughts and ideas regarding video games, comics, anime, movies, etc.
I am not a professional, or even a connoisseur of the aforementioned subjects.
Despite that, I want to learn more about the content I consume, and have an outlet
to express how I feel about it.
